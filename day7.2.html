Monaco Grand Prix Highlight

In 2018, Lewis Hamilton, a legendary F1 racer, made history by pulling off a triple overtake in Practice 1, paving his way straight to the podium. The Monaco Grand Prix is renowned for its challenging street circuit and glamorous atmosphere, making it a favorite among fans and drivers alike.

  • Location: Monte Carlo, Monaco
  • Famous for: Tight corners, luxury, and celebrity attendance
  • 2018 Winner: Daniel Ricciardo
  • Notable Moment: Lewis Hamilton's triple overtake in Practice 1
